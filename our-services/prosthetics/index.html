Dental Crowns

Dental crowns are custom-made caps placed over a tooth that is severely decayed, damaged, or has undergone root canal treatment. They fully cover the visible portion of the tooth, restoring its shape, size, strength, and appearance. With proper care, they offer a long-lasting and functional solution.

Dental Bridges

Dental bridges are fixed prosthetic solutions used to replace one or more missing teeth. They consist of one or more artificial teeth (pontics) held in place by crowns on adjacent healthy teeth or dental implants. Bridges not only restore your smile but also prevent surrounding teeth from shifting out of place, which can lead to bite problems and further tooth loss.
